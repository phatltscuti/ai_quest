3. Detailed Setup: The 3-Stage Automation Flow

Stage 1: Filtering "Actionable" Emails

The first step is pinpointing the high-potential emails. We use the Gmail Starter with the following advanced query:

in:inbox is:unread -from:aiquest.com subject:("AI Expo" OR "Collaboration" OR "Pricing")

Stage 2: Contextual Reasoning (Gemini)

This is the heart of the agent. AI goes beyond templates; it reasons based on data. Here is the standardized English Prompt for maximum accuracy:

"You are a professional Business Development Assistant. 
Analyze the provided email thread history.
Your Task: Generate a contextual reply draft that:
1. Greets the sender and references the meeting at AI Expo 2026.
2. Summarizes previous discussions and addresses all technical/pricing questions.
3. Suggests a clear next action (e.g., a 15-minute demo call).
4. Includes 3 intelligent clarifying questions to ensure no information is missed.
Tone: Professional, helpful, and concise.
Constraint: DO NOT hallucinate facts not present in the thread."

Stage 3: Staging the Action (Gmail Draft)

Finalize the process by saving the content to Gmail Drafts and applying the Draft-created label for easy visibility.

4. Deep Dive: Thread Awareness with Gemini in Gmail

Why choose Gemini over traditional automated responders? The answer lies in "Thread Awareness." An effective response must be a seamless continuation of the conversation that came before.

Contextual Intelligence (Thread Integration)

Within Workspace Studio, Gemini can access the Thread history object. It can distinguish between "Lead A" (who agreed on pricing but is worried about security) and "Lead B" (who is just starting their research). The "Clarifying Questions" generated by the AI allow the Sales team to bypass trivial Q&A and jump straight to closing the deal.

Accuracy Assurance (Quality Gate)

Statement in Draft Snippet from Source Thread Verification
"Glad we met at booth #5""I visited your booth (#5) at the Expo..."✅ Match
"Regarding the API integration...""Does your system support REST API?"✅ Match
"Sending the Enterprise quote""Interested in Enterprise tier pricing."✅ Match
"Meeting next Tuesday morning""Let's catch up next Tuesday morning."✅ Match
"Noted your current CRM is Salesforce""We are currently using Salesforce..."✅ Match

5. Exception Handling: When AI Steps Back

Operational safety is our top priority. We implement a specific logical branch (Conditional Branching) to handle sensitive subjects.

EXCEPTION LOGIC

If the email subject or body contains keywords like: Complaint, Legal, or Negotiation.

Result: AI halts drafting. Instead, it sends a Google Chat Alert to the senior management space with a direct link to the email for immediate human intervention.
