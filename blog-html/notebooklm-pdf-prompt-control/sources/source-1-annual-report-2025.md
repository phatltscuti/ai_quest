# EduNova Annual Report 2025

## Company Overview

**EduNova** is a Vietnamese EdTech company founded in 2020, headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City. Our mission is to democratize quality education through AI-powered technology. We serve over 2.1 million active learners across Vietnam, Thailand, and Indonesia.

**Industry:** Education Technology (EdTech)
**Founded:** 2020
**Headquarters:** Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
**Employees:** 420 (as of December 2025)

## Financial Highlights 2025

### Revenue

| Metric | 2024 | 2025 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Total Revenue | $18.2M | $28.7M | +57.7% |
| B2C Revenue (Individual Learners) | $9.1M | $13.5M | +48.4% |

### Key Financial Metrics

- **Gross Margin:** 72.3% (up from 68.1% in 2024)
- **Operating Expenses:** $22.1M (R&D: 42%, Sales & Marketing: 31%, G&A: 27%)
- **Net Loss:** -$2.8M (narrowed from -$5.1M in 2024)
- **Cash Position:** $15.3M (after Series B round)
- **Burn Rate:** ~$230K/month (reduced from $425K/month in 2024)
- **Runway:** 5.5 years at current burn rate

### Revenue Breakdown by Segment

**B2C (Individual Learners) — 47% of revenue:**
- 1.4 million paid subscribers (up 52% YoY)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU): $9.64/month
- Churn rate: 6.2% monthly (improved from 8.7%)

**B2B (Schools & Institutions) — 38% of revenue:**
- 340 partner schools (up from 180 in 2024)
- Average contract value: $31,800/year
- Renewal rate: 89%

**Enterprise (Corporate Training) — 15% of revenue:**
- 45 enterprise clients (up from 22)
- Average deal size: $97,800/year
- Net Revenue Retention: 124%

- 85,000 beta users in first 3 months

- Offline mode for areas with limited connectivity

## Key Risks

1. **Regulatory Risk:** Vietnam and Thailand tightening AI in education regulations; EduNova actively participates in policy working groups
2. **Competition:** Increased competition from Ruangguru (Indonesia), Topica (Vietnam), and potential entry by Duolingo into SEA market
3. **Data Privacy:** Handling student data (including minors) requires strict PDPA/PDPD compliance; invested $1.2M in security infrastructure
4. **Talent Retention:** AI/ML engineers in high demand; implemented equity compensation and flexible work policy (attrition reduced to 12% from 22%)
