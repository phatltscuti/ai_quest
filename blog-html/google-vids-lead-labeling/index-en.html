3. The Generative Workflow with Standardized Prompts

Step 1: The "Multi-Scene" Strategy (3-Min Goal)

To reach a 3-minute video (180 seconds) with 8-second scenes, you need around 22-23 independent segments. Use the following English prompts in the Veo sidebar to build each part:

Segment (8s each) English Prompt (Copy & Paste to Veo)
1. Intro"Cinematic intro: Welcome to the CRM Lead Labeling training."
2. Definition"What is a CRM and why is it our source of truth?"
3. Highlight"The importance of lead data accuracy for business success."
4. Problem"Show a messy database with unorganized and lost leads."
5. Consequence"Sales reps losing opportunities due to poor data quality."
6. Solution"Introduction to the Professional Lead Labeling System."
7. Category: Hot"Definition of 'Hot' leads: Fast action required icons."
8. Category: Warm"Definition of 'Warm' leads: Nurturing required icons."
9. Category: Cold"Definition of 'Cold' leads: Long-term follow up icons."
10-20. Technical DemoDetailed prompts for: "Find Status field", "Click Hot label", "Click Save", "Check CRM sync"...
21-23. Outro"Summary of benefits: Clean reports and higher sales conversion."

5. Workflow Analysis: AI Accept vs. Human Edit

In this project, we don't let AI do 100%. Here is how we optimize the editing workflow:

Video Component Status (AI vs. Human) Rationale & Adjustments
Storyboard Structure 90% Accept AI organizes chapters logically; only minor title renaming was needed.
Illustrative Imagery 40% Change Replaced some generic stock with real company CRM screenshots.
Voiceover 100% Accept AI voices are highly natural, saving hours of manual recording.
Instructive Content Manual Capture AI cannot access internal systems; live demos must use Recording Studio.

6. Publishing & Knowledge Maintenance

The final video is managed centrally:

  • Storage Location: Google Drive > Shared Drives > Sales_Enablement > Training_Videos.
  • Access Control: Set to "Viewer" for the org, and "Content Manager" for the L&D team.
  • Updates: When CRM rules change, simply reopen the Vids project, swap the demo scene, and save. The sharing link remains identical!
